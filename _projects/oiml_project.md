Open Infrastructure for Machine Learning (OIML) is a project of the WOGRA AG 
research group in cooperation with the German Aerospace Center and is 
funded by the Ministry of Economic Affairs, Regional Development and 
Energy as part of the High Tech Agenda of the Free State of Bavaria. The  
project duration is 3 years with a budget of 1.3 Mio EUR.

My job is to supervise Open space for Machine Learning (Os4ML), an open 
source platform for automated machine learning, as product owner and 
scientific advisor. The goal is to enable non-experts to solve every day 
problems with AI.

Follow these links for more details:

- [GitHub](https://github.com/WOGRA-AG/Os4ML)
